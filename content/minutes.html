Editor: This page posts the month and date of the board minutes as a clickable link.

One method is to simply link to an uploaded pdf version of the minutes.

An alternative method is to create a page and fill in the link address (URL) in the text block. Do one or the other, but not both.

Creating a page is done in admin at Pages > Minutes-Monthly > choose year > Add Subpage. Follow the instructions there.

To start an entirely new year, click the "Save and Add another" button at the end of this page.

Troubleshooting: If the month/year link appears twice on the page, it is because both the pdf link-to-file and the page URL have been entered. Eliminate one or the other- the typical choice would be to eliminate the pdf.
