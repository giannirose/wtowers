Editor: On this page we post the board minutes.

Know where the file is on the computer.
Follow the "Select and Upload a File" directions and the pdf wil be available on the page.
Click the "Save" button.

There is the option to enter text that will appear in the given month, but this is rarely needed.

There is a "Save and Add another" button at the top and bottom of the page. Clicking either and saving will start a new year with the column of months laid out. The year can be filled in.

There is an alternative way to make a web page of the minutes. This is explained on the Editing Instructions page. The advantage is that the text has the look of the website and is searchable.

Troubleshooting: If the month/year link appears twice on the page, it is because both the pdf link-to-file and the page URL have been entered. The typical solution is to delete the "Select or Upload a File" link.
